Projects

Tactile Sensor Design for Fabric Detection   ◆   Research

Project Advisors: Yajing Shen

In this project, we are working to develop a high-precision tactile sensor and propose a feature-based detection algorithm for non-destructive sensing to support the interaction with soft, deformable surfaces, based on experimental evaluations of a robotic arm system grasping single-layer fabrics.

Using Wearable Devices and Cameras for Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) Screening   ◆   Research

Project Advisors: Xiaomin Ouyang

In this project, we are working to develop a privacy-aware tracking architecture and propose advanced re-identification algorithms to support continuous ASD diagnostic screening in public environments, based on leveraging synergistic data from wearable devices and cameras to capture distinctive behavioral markers in early childhood.

Inspired by my firsthand experiences with autistic children, this research aims to bridge the gap between clinical observation and real-world screening.
